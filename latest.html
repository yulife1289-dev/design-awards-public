年份

類別
Residential – Single-Family Detached

A solitary 3,360-sqft dwelling embedded in an 8,000-acre tallgrass prairie restoration in North Dakota's Prairie Pothole Region. The emphatically horizontal form centres on a sheltered courtyard; a continuous green roof extends the prairie across the structure. Envelope of concrete, black-oxide stainless steel, and glass endures scorching summers and frigid winters.

年份

類別
Self-Build Residential

RIBA House of the Year 2025. A self-built timber-frame house clad in Lewisian Gneiss, wrapping at 135 degrees around existing rock formations for shelter. Named 'little quiet one by the rock' in Gaelic, delivered on a tight budget by architect-owners with local contractors. Unanimously awarded for climatic response, material honesty, and embodied restraint. Photography by Richard Gaston.

建築師
Patkau Architects
地點
Victoria, BC, Canada
年份
2026
類別
Residential – Single-Family Detached

A 6,000-sqft pavilion-like dwelling overlooking Cadboro Bay, Victoria. A rhythmic pleated wooden arbour ceiling suspended below arrays of operable skylights coheres the interior. Retractable glass walls, geothermal heat pumps, and 60 rooftop solar panels achieve exemplary energy performance. Patkau Architects' fifth AIA Architecture Award. Photography by James Dow.
