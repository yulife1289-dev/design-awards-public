案例背景

Located within the landscape of a scenic golf course, Residence Porto was conceived to establish a harmonious relationship between architecture and nature. Positioned slightly above the access street, the residence takes advantage of its subtle elevation to expand views across the surrounding greenery, creating a sense of openness despite the flat terrain. Its L-shaped layout organizes the program around outdoor living spaces, strengthening the integration between interior environments and the landscape while encouraging natural light and cross ventilation throughout the house.
